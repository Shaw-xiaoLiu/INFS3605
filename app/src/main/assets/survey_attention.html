Attention


Personal and sensitive information


Personal and sensitive user data includes personally identifiable information, authentication information cameras sensor data and the survey result submitted by users.


When using this application, your information will be collected and shared with The New South Wales Ministry of Health ( NSW Health). The information will only be used for screening and tracking COVD-19 and will not be used by third parties.


Legal responsibility


The application works closely with relevant government agencies. The survey you take is important for NSW COVID-19 tracking and public health, so please provide true information. Intentionally submitting misleading information will be considered a violation of public health. Serious cases will receive punishment.
